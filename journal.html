Our History

A journey through time

The NOIR Story

In the bustling streets of Paris in 2010, NOIR was born from a simple vision: to create clothing that transcends trends, embracing the purity of monochrome design. Founded by visionary designer Elena Noir, the brand began in a small atelier where Elena handcrafted each piece, drawing inspiration from the city's timeless architecture and the stark beauty of black and white photography.

As word spread, NOIR's commitment to sustainable materials and ethical production caught the eye of fashion influencers. By 2015, our first collection debuted at Paris Fashion Week, receiving acclaim for its minimalist elegance. We expanded to include essential wardrobe staples, always staying true to our core palette.

Through challenges like the global pandemic, NOIR pivoted to online experiences, hosting virtual atelier tours and collaborating with artists worldwide. Today, in 2025, NOIR stands as a symbol of enduring style, with boutiques in major cities and a community of loyal patrons who appreciate the art of simplicity.

Our history is one of passion, perseverance, and purity – a black and white tale in a colorful world.
